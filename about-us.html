About Us

Greetings! Welcome to Orra, where innovation meets heartfelt connection. We're D.J. (Instagram: @dpotterx, Facebook: dpotterx) and David (Instagram: @daiderza), two friends who met during our grad school days and eventually joined forces to create something truly special: Orra, the next generation dating app designed to revolutionize the way men and women find true, meaningful love in the digital age.

Our journey started with a simple observation: the current dating landscape is a mess. Swipe-based dating apps have left millions of individuals disillusioned and longing for real, deep emotional connection. Additionally, many believe that swipe-based dating apps such as Tinder, Hinge and Bumble have contributed to the loneliness epidemic. Determined to make a difference, we set out to build Orra, a platform that prioritizes authenticity, evolutionary psychology, and genuine human connection.

We believe Orra is the world’s most advanced AI-driven matchmaking technology. Unlike traditional swipe-centric or algorithm-based apps, Orra harnesses the power of a cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Orra’s AI model also pulls from millennia of evolutionary psychology to pair users with statistically compatible partners, fostering relationships that have the potential to endure the test of time.

We're thrilled to announce that Orra will be making its debut in early April, and we couldn't be more excited to share our creation with you. Whether you're seeking your soulmate or simply curious about what Orra has to offer, we invite you to join us on this journey towards real love, deep connection, and true fulfillment.

For more information and updates, be sure to visit our website at www.orra.co. Get ready to discover love, the Orra way!
